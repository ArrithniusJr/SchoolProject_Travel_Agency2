Royale Hills Lodge & Spa

Royal Hills Lodge and Spa offers bed and breakfast and is located in Thohoyandou. The lodge is the ideal base for a comfortable stopover for guests, en route to various attractions. Accommodation at Royal Hills Lodge and Spa consists of 10 individually decorated rooms.

Book Now

Stay Guest Hotel

The Style Guest House offers a tranquil getaway right in the heart of Thohoyandou in the Limpopo Province. The guest house is located directly behind the town's picturesque botanical garden, secluded from the bustle of the town.

Book Now

Anita Guest House

Anisa Guest House is an owner-managed guest house offering offering genuine hospitality and warmth, with an easy modernist African style. The guest house is set in a tranquil environment in Thohoyandou Block Q, conveniently close to town, shopping areas and business, government offices, Thavhani Mall, and the University of Venda.

Book Now

Vhafamadi Bed & Breakfast

Vhafamadi Bed And Breakfast is a guest house that is located in Makwarela township, which is a few kilometers from Thohoyandou city center. Ideal for guests looking for comfortable lodging, for either a leisure or corporate breakaway.

Book Now

Masili Guest House

Masili Guesthouse offers guest rooms and is situated in the town of Sibasa; approximately 5km central Thohoyandou. Accommodation at Masili Guesthouse comprises 9 individually decorated rooms. These rooms are furnished with either twin beds, a double bed or a queen-size bed, and each room has an en-suite bathroom.
